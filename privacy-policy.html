ZK-Proof Privacy — Your Data Never Leaves Your Device

When you complete the liveness scan for KYC, IN$DEX uses Zero-Knowledge Proof technology to verify your identity without ever seeing or storing your biometric data.

🔐 How ZK-Proof Works

Think of it like proving you're over 18 without showing your ID. Your device generates a cryptographic proof — a mathematical certificate — that confirms "this is a real, live person" without revealing anything about who that person is. We receive the proof. We never receive the data.

This means: even if IN$DEX's servers were ever compromised, your biometric data would not be at risk — because we simply don't have it.

For transfers above $1,000 USD, the FATF Travel Rule requires us to share sender/recipient identification with counterparty Virtual Asset Service Providers (VASPs). We do this via TRISA/OpenVASP protocols — again using ZK-proofs where possible, so the minimum necessary data is shared and no more.

Your Grid Account — No Seed Phrase, No Back Door

Your Grid Account is a Squads Protocol v4 Multisig wallet, secured by 2-of-3 MPC (Multi-Party Computation) keys.

Key 1
Your device (TEE-secured, never exported)
Key 2
Your Guardian (person you nominate during setup)
Key 3
IN$DEX recovery (used only in verified recovery flow — never unilaterally)

Any two of the three keys are needed to authorise a transaction. IN$DEX alone cannot move your funds. There is no seed phrase — which means there is nothing to steal, photograph, or guess.

IN$DEX's Key 3 is held in a TEE-backed HSM and is used only when you initiate a verified recovery flow. We log every use of Key 3 and it is visible to MemeDAO governance.
