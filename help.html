Glossary
FAQ
C
Civilisation Fund Governance
A shared community treasury that receives 2% of every transaction on IN$DEX. It funds infrastructure like mesh nodes, education, insurance reserves, and ambassador rewards. Citizens vote on how it is spent. Think of it like a community rates fund — but run entirely by the community.
Compressed NFT Receipt Marketplace
Every purchase on IN$DEX creates a permanent, tamper-proof digital receipt stored on the Solana blockchain. It costs less than a fraction of a cent to create and serves as immutable proof of ownership and transaction.
D
DEX Wallet
Decentralised Exchange. A place to swap tokens (like INDX, SOL, USDC) directly with other people — no bank or middleman required. IN$DEX's DEX runs on the Solana blockchain, meaning trades settle in under a second for less than a cent.
E
Escrow Marketplace
When you buy something on IN$DEX, your payment is held in escrow — locked by a smart contract on Solana. The seller can't touch it until you confirm you received your item. If there's a dispute, the funds stay locked until it's resolved. It's buyer protection built into the code itself.
See how it works in Order Status →
G
Genesis Bonus Earn
A one-time gift of 1,000 INDX (worth $350 USD) awarded to every citizen who completes Sovereign Verification during the launch period. It rewards early adopters who help build the Republic.
Grid Account Wallet
Your IN$DEX wallet — but unlike a regular crypto wallet, you never need to write down a "seed phrase" (a long list of random words you could lose). Instead, your wallet is secured by three keys split across your device, the cloud, and a recovery option. You only need 2 of the 3 to access your funds — so losing your phone doesn't mean losing your money.
Manage your Grid Account keys →
Governance Governance
Citizens with a Wisdom Score of 50 or above can vote on proposals that change how IN$DEX works — fee adjustments, new features, language support, and more. Voting power is proportional to your Wisdom Score, not how much money you have.
Go to Governance →
I
INDX Token Wallet
The currency of IN$DEX. Built on Solana (SPL Token-2022). Genesis price is $0.35 USD per token. You use INDX to buy goods, pay for services, stake for yield, and participate in governance. There are 100 million INDX total — no more can ever be created.

IN$DEX Domain (.IN$DEX) Wallet
Your human-readable identity on the platform — like aj.IN$DEX. Instead of sharing a long wallet address (e.g. 7xKz…mN4p), anyone can send you tokens just by typing your name. Think of it like an email address for your money.
L
Light Node Earn
A small software program that runs on your phone or computer, helping to relay transactions across the IN$DEX Sovereign Mesh network. In return, you earn 3–8 INDX per day passively. You don't need special hardware — just a phone with a stable connection.
Set up a Light Node →
R
98/2 Law Marketplace
The core economic rule of IN$DEX: on every transaction, 98% goes directly to the seller or creator, and 2% goes to the Civilisation Fund. There are no hidden fees, no bank cut, no platform markup. The code enforces this automatically — it cannot be changed without a citizen vote.
RWA (Real World Asset) Marketplace
A physical item or property that has been tokenised — turned into a digital token on the Solana blockchain. This lets you own a provable share of something real (land, art, a business) using INDX, with your ownership recorded permanently on-chain.
S
SIINDEX AI
Sovereign Intelligent INDEX — the AI agent swarm that acts as IN$DEX's Chief Operating Officer. SIINDEX monitors transactions, flags anomalies, supports citizens 24/7 via the Support Agent, and generates daily operations briefings for the Wisdom Council. It is not a chatbot — it is an autonomous agent system built on Claude AI.
Chat with SIINDEX Support →
Sovereign Delivery Marketplace
A peer-to-peer courier network run by IN$DEX citizens. Instead of using a courier company, community members deliver packages to each other and earn INDX for each delivery. It keeps money inside the community and works even in areas without traditional courier services.
Sovereign Mesh Wallet
A decentralised internet layer that lets IN$DEX operate even when standard internet is unavailable — in rural areas, during outages, or in regions with internet restrictions. Light Nodes form the mesh. Offline payments sync automatically when connectivity is restored.
Sovereign Verification Governance
A tiered identity system with three levels: Citizen (phone number), Verified Citizen (government ID), and Sovereign Citizen (community vouching + biometrics). Higher tiers unlock higher transaction limits and the Genesis Bonus. Your documents are verified but never stored by IN$DEX.

Sovereign Yield Earn
Interest earned by locking (staking) your INDX for a period of time. Rates: 4% (flexible), 5.5% (30 days), 7.2% (90 days), 10% (365 days). Requires Wisdom Score 100 to unlock. The yield comes from a portion of the Civilisation Fund and transaction fees.

W
Wisdom Score Governance
A reputation score (0–200) that grows as you contribute to the Republic. It starts at 20 when you join and increases as you verify your identity, buy and sell, vote on proposals, refer friends, and stake INDX. It is not based on how much money you have — it measures your contribution. Higher scores unlock features: 50 = governance, 100 = Sovereign Yield, 150 = fee reduction, 200 = Sovereign Elder.
View your Wisdom Score →
Wisdom Council Governance
Citizens with a Wisdom Score of 150+ who form IN$DEX's governing body. They review governance proposals, mediate escalated disputes, and hold the keys to constitutional amendments. Membership is earned, not appointed.
Do I need a bank account to use IN$DEX? +
No. IN$DEX is built specifically for people without bank accounts. You can buy INDX with cash at a participating merchant, receive payments from other citizens, and buy goods — all without ever touching a bank. Your Grid Account wallet is your bank.
What happens if I lose my phone? +
Your Grid Account is secured by 3 keys (Device, Cloud, Recovery). You only need 2 of 3 to access your funds. If you lose your phone, your Cloud Key (in iCloud or Google Drive) and Recovery Key are enough to restore full access from a new device. No seed phrase required. See security settings →
Is my money safe when I buy something? +
Yes. Every purchase uses escrow — your INDX is locked in a smart contract on Solana until you confirm your item arrived. The seller cannot access the funds until you release them, or until 7 days pass with no dispute. If something goes wrong, you can open a dispute and the funds stay locked until it's resolved.
How do I earn INDX without buying it? +
Several ways: Sell goods on the marketplace (+INDX per sale), run a Light Node (3–8 INDX/day passively), refer friends (+10 Wisdom Score, bonus INDX), complete verification (Genesis Bonus of 1,000 INDX), or stake INDX to earn 4–10% yield. You can also earn by delivering packages via Sovereign Delivery.
What is Wisdom Score and why does it matter? +
Wisdom Score is your reputation on IN$DEX. It starts at 20 when you join and grows with every contribution — buying, selling, verifying, voting, referring. It is not about wealth. A citizen with 10 INDX and WS 80 has more governance power than one with 10,000 INDX and WS 30. Key milestones: WS 50 = vote on proposals, WS 100 = unlock Sovereign Yield, WS 150 = fee reduction + Wisdom Council eligibility.
Why does IN$DEX use Solana? +
Solana processes transactions in under a second for less than $0.01. This makes it practical for everyday commerce — unlike Ethereum, where fees can exceed the value of small purchases. IN$DEX uses Solana's SPL Token-2022 standard and Metaplex Bubblegum for compressed NFT receipts (each costs ~$0.000005 to mint).
How does Sovereign Delivery work? +
When a seller selects "Sovereign Delivery", they mark the package ready for collection. IN$DEX notifies nearby citizen couriers who can claim the delivery. The courier picks it up, delivers it, and earns INDX on delivery confirmation. No courier company needed — the community delivers to itself.
What is the Civilisation Fund and who controls it? +
The Civilisation Fund receives 2% of every transaction on IN$DEX. It is controlled collectively by all citizens through governance votes. Currently it funds mesh node infrastructure, sovereign education, the insurance reserve, and ambassador rewards. No individual controls it — decisions require a majority citizen vote.
Can I convert INDX back to regular money (AUD, USD etc.)? +
Yes. You can swap INDX for USDC (a stablecoin pegged to the US Dollar) via the DEX Swap screen, then withdraw USDC to a bank or exchange. The process takes a few minutes. Tier 1 KYC verification is required for fiat withdrawals above $3,200/day. Go to DEX Swap →
What if I have a problem with a purchase? +
You have 7 days after dispatch to open a dispute. Go to Order Status → Open Dispute. Your escrow funds stay locked while the dispute is active. Simple disputes are resolved by SIINDEX automatically. Complex cases go to the Sovereign Court, where a random jury of high-Wisdom citizens reviews evidence and votes on the outcome. See Buyer Protection →
Is IN$DEX available offline? +
Partially. The Sovereign Mesh mode lets you send and receive INDX payments peer-to-peer over Bluetooth/WiFi-Direct when there's no internet. Transactions are cryptographically signed on-device and broadcast to the Solana network once connectivity is restored. See Offline Mode →
