# Mama Noe

**Role:** ICP Avatar — Ideal Citizen Profile
**Relationship:** Foundational design constraint. The "why" of the entire IN$DEX build.
**Contact:** N/A (composite persona)
**Notes:** 
- 80-year-old woman, Mele Village, Vanuatu
- 50% smartphone confidence
- No bank account
- Sells tuluk (traditional food) for $1.50
- Represents the billions excluded from digital finance

**The Mama Noe Test:** If she can use a feature without explanation, it works. If she can't, rebuild it.

**Why she matters:**
IN$DEX is built for sovereign financial access for everyone — Mama Noe is the hardest case. If the product passes her test, it works for every citizen on earth.
