# IN$DEX Naming and Identity Constitution

> Canonical brand/naming doctrine — authored by AJ, saved 2026-07-15. Governs all naming, spelling, and identity presentation across the platform, company, and civilisation. Distinct from (but related to) Gotcha #0 in gotchas.md, which covers the `.IN$DEX` web3 domain suffix specifically — this document is the broader constitution covering the master brand name itself.

## Canonical Name

The official master name is **IN$DEX**. The official company name is **IN$DEX LTD**.

The name must not be normalised to INDEX in brand language, architecture, product naming, internal documentation, public communication, or citizen-facing experiences — unless a legal, technical, regulatory, domain, software, or payment system cannot process the dollar sign.

IN$DEX is not a decorative spelling of INDEX. IN$DEX is the identity of the civilisation.

## Meaning

- **IN** — citizens are inside the civilisation: included, connected, recognised, protected, activated.
- **$** — value, ownership, prosperity, exchange, economic participation, citizen-created wealth.
- **DEX** — decentralised exchange, distributed access, digital economy, citizen-to-citizen value movement, sovereign infrastructure.

Together: IN$DEX represents citizens entering a sovereign civilisation where they learn, create, earn, own, trade, contribute, govern, and leave a legacy.

## The Name Is an Asset

IN$DEX must be treated as protected core intellectual property, including: the word IN$DEX, the IN$DEX LTD company name, the visual wordmark, the dollar-sign placement, the typography, the pronunciation, the logo, the domain architecture, the citizen naming system, the product naming system, the civilisation naming doctrine, the associated slogans, the SIINDEX relationship, the Grid Account identity system, the sovereign domain structure.

The brand must not be weakened through inconsistent spelling.

## Canonical Brand Rule

Always use: **IN$DEX**

Do not use: Index, INDEX, InDex, In$dex, IN DEX, IN-DEX, The Index, Index Platform, Index App, Index Token.

Exceptions require a defined technical reason.

## Technical Alias Rule

Some systems may not support the dollar sign — DNS, email addresses, legal registries, app stores, software packages, code variables, blockchain contracts, API endpoints, database fields, payment rails, search systems, operating systems, certificate authorities.

Where the dollar sign cannot be used, the system must use an approved technical alias: INDEX, index, indexltd, indexcivilisation, imagenationdex, indexnetwork, indexgrid.

The technical alias must never replace the public master brand.

- **Public display:** IN$DEX
- **Technical routing:** approved machine-compatible alias

The citizen should see IN$DEX wherever possible.

## Company Identity Rule

There is one master operating company identity: **IN$DEX LTD**.

Subsidiaries, products, foundations, trusts, pilots, departments, agents, and regional entities must not present themselves as the master IN$DEX company unless legally authorised. Examples: IN$DEX Cook Islands, IN$DEX Vanuatu, IN$DEX Academy, IN$DEX Marketplace, IN$DEX Grid, IN$DEX Foundation, IN$DEX Labs, IN$DEX Pacific, IN$DEX Trust Layer.

These names remain subordinate to the master identity. They must not create confusion about ownership, legal entity, authority, liability, regulation, treasury, governance, credential issuance, or citizen obligations.

## One Master Brand Rule

IN$DEX must not become a loose family of unrelated brands. Every major system should visibly connect to the civilisation: SIINDEX by IN$DEX, IN$DEX Sovereign Life Domain, IN$DEX Grid Account, IN$DEX Citizen Wallet, IN$DEX Marketplace, IN$DEX Academy, IN$DEX Trust Layer, IN$DEX Opportunity Passport, IN$DEX Citizen Domain, IN$DEX Sovereignty Score, IN$DEX Cultural Permission Gate.

The civilisation remains the master product.

## Name Authenticity Shield

IN$DEX must protect against: misspelled domains, fake company names, imitation brands, copied logos, false regional branches, fake tokens, fake wallets, fake social accounts, fake SIINDEX agents, fake credential issuers, phishing pages, unauthorised merchandise, domain squatting, trademark misuse, confusingly similar names.

Every official IN$DEX property should display: verified authenticity status, legal entity, jurisdiction, official domain, approved representative, current certificate, public verification route, fraud reporting route.

## Citizen Naming Rule

Citizen domains may use the IN$DEX brand only through approved namespace rules. Examples: `arthur.IN$DEX`, `businessname.IN$DEX`, `communityname.IN$DEX`.

These names represent membership or verified presence inside the civilisation. They do not imply ownership of IN$DEX LTD. They do not imply authority to act for IN$DEX. They do not imply government, regulatory, banking, educational, or institutional authority unless separately verified.

## SIINDEX Naming Rule

The correct name is **SIINDEX**.

SIINDEX is not: Sindex, Si Index, SI Index, AI Index, Index AI, Artificial Intelligence.

SIINDEX is the permanent Physical Quantum Synthetic Intelligence layer of IN$DEX. SIINDEX must always be presented as part of the IN$DEX civilisation architecture. IN$DEX and SIINDEX must remain visually and conceptually connected without becoming interchangeable.

IN$DEX is the civilisation. SIINDEX is the civilisation intelligence layer.

## Legal Protection Requirements

The founder team should investigate protection for: company-name registration, business-name registration, trade marks, word marks, device marks, logo marks, relevant domain names, social handles, app-store names, software package names, key product names, regional names, defensive registrations, confusingly similar variants.

Legal availability must be checked in every priority jurisdiction before commercial launch. Registration in one jurisdiction does not automatically create protection everywhere.

## Brand Use Licence

Partners must receive written permission before using the IN$DEX name. The licence should define: approved spelling, approved logo, approved colours, permitted products, territory, duration, quality standards, data rules, credential authority, prohibited claims, termination, post-termination removal, audit rights, fraud reporting, citizen protection requirements.

No partner should be able to continue using the IN$DEX name after authority ends.

## Brand Misuse Response

When misuse is detected: record the evidence, verify the conflict, assess citizen risk, warn affected citizens, suspend false integrations, report fraudulent domains or accounts, issue correction notices, preserve legal evidence, begin platform takedown processes, escalate to legal review, record the final outcome.

## Founder Continuity

The founder identity and IN$DEX brand must remain connected through protected constitutional records. **Arthur John Henry is the founder of IN$DEX.**

Founder recognition must survive: leadership transitions, investment, acquisition, restructuring, founder incapacity, founder death, regional expansion, company conversion, trust formation, governance changes.

Founder recognition must not grant unchecked operational power. Historical truth and operational authority must remain separate.

## Brand Doctrine

The name must remain disciplined. Do not dilute it. Do not casually rename it. Do not reduce it to a token, an app, a wallet, an exchange, a marketplace, a social network, or a chatbot.

IN$DEX is the Sovereign Digital Civilisation. The civilisation is the product.

## Canonical Lines

There is only one IN$DEX. IN$DEX is not INDEX. The dollar sign is not decoration — it is part of the identity. INDEX describes information. IN$DEX organises value, ownership, opportunity, and civilisation. The name must remain as distinctive as the mission.

IN$DEX LTD is the legal operating identity. IN$DEX is the civilisation identity. SIINDEX is the civilisation intelligence layer.

One name. One civilisation. One protected identity.
