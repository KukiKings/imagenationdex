1. Strategic Foundation

What the website must do

IN$DEX is not a typical fintech product. The website serves three very different audiences simultaneously: the unbanked Pacific Islander who may never have used a financial app, the diaspora sender looking for a cheaper way to send money home, and the investor or press who needs to understand the business case quickly. The website must speak plainly to all three without dumbing down the technology or intimidating the newcomer.

The Mama Noe Test
Every page must pass this test: a Pacific Island grandmother can land on any page, understand what IN$DEX is and what she should do next, within 2 minutes with no one explaining it to her. If she's confused, the page fails.
Primary Conversion Goal (Pre-Launch)
Every page has one job before 24 January 2027: get the visitor on the waitlist. Every other goal is secondary. The site architecture reinforces this relentlessly "Join the Sovereign Republic" appears on every page, always leading to /join.
Origin Story as Conversion Tool
AJ's origin story the coconut girl in the Cook Islands who couldn't make change for $50 is the most powerful conversion element on the site. It goes on the homepage above the fold, and echoes through every mission page. Real stories beat product features every time.

7. CTA Strategy

Every page has one primary action never two

The biggest mistake in financial product websites is giving visitors too many choices. IN$DEX follows a strict CTA hierarchy: one primary action per page, one secondary action, and a tertiary for edge cases. Every CTA changes based on the user's phase.

Primary CTA Pre-launch
Join the Sovereign Republic
→ /join
Appears on: every page. Blue-to-purple gradient. Always the rightmost nav item.
Primary CTA Post-launch
Start Takes 90 Seconds
→ /onboard (terms-of-service)
Replaces waitlist CTA on L99 launch day. Same button position, new destination.
Secondary CTA Always
Open My Wallet
→ /app (app-lock.html)
Ghost button. Top nav only. For existing citizens who already onboarded.
Tertiary CTA Social
Gift INDX to a Friend
→ /app/gift
Appears in dashboard and fee schedule. Drives viral referral loop.
Trust CTA Compliance pages
See How We Keep You Safe
→ /compliance
Secondary option on Home and Token pages. Reassures the cautious visitor.
Mission CTA Why pages
Read AJ's Story
→ /about or /why
Tertiary link in hero. Converts the emotionally-driven visitor before the feature-driven one.
