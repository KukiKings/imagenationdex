## The 7 Canonical Laws of Sovereign Social

These are not features. They are laws. Every build decision must honour all seven.

### Law 1 — The 98/2 Creator Law
The creator keeps **98% of every earning** from every interaction: SovTokens, likes, subscriptions, view rewards, live tips, content sales. 2% goes to the Civilisation Fund. IN$DEX keeps 0%. This is immutable. It cannot be changed by governance, by SIINDEX, or by AJ. It is hardcoded.

**Comparison:** YouTube keeps 30%. TikTok keeps ~70% effective. IN$DEX keeps **zero**.

**SovTokens** are the IN$DEX tipping and super-interaction mechanism — powered by INDX micro-payments.

- Viewers send SovTokens directly to creators during live streams, on posts, and in response to content
- SovTokens pin a message to the top of a live stream (equivalent to YouTube Super Chat)
- SovTokens trigger animated on-screen reactions (animated Pacific-themed emojis and motifs)
- SovToken tiers:

| Sovereign | 50 INDX | $12.00 | Pinned message, 10 minutes |
| Elder | 200 INDX | $48.00 | Pinned message, 1 hour, gold frame |

- 98/2 applies to every SovToken — creator receives 98%, 2% to Civilisation Fund, 0% to platform
- SIINDEX confirms every SovToken transaction with a Verifiable Receipt NFT

### Law 3 — Content as Verifiable Receipt NFT
Every piece of content published on Sovereign Social is **automatically minted as a Verifiable Receipt NFT** at the moment of publication.

- The NFT records: creator wallet, timestamp, content hash, platform, format type
- The creator owns the NFT — not IN$DEX, not the platform
- If the creator leaves IN$DEX, they keep their NFT — proof of authorship, forever, on-chain
- Content NFTs are tradeable — a creator can sell their viral post as a collectible
- Content NFT metadata is stored on Arweave (permanent, decentralised)
- SIINDEX issues the NFT silently — the citizen doesn't need to understand NFTs. It just happens.

### Law 4 — No Algorithm. SIINDEX Curates.
There is no engagement-bait algorithm on Sovereign Social. No dark pattern. No rage-bait amplification. No data selling.

SIINDEX curates based on:
- **Pacific First** — Pacific nations content is amplified by default
- **Wisdom Score** — higher Wisdom Score creators are surfaced more (earned, not bought)
- **Cultural relevance** — language, location, community signals
- **Community values** — content that earns genuine engagement from real citizens, not bots
- **Time-based fairness** — new creators get equal initial exposure; earned reach builds over time

**What SIINDEX never does:**
- Never sells citizen attention to advertisers
- Never surfaces rage content because it drives engagement
- Never suppresses creators because they didn't pay for promotion
- Never sells creator audience data to third parties

Creators own their audience list. It is stored in their Grid Account. The platform cannot access it without consent.

*(Rates subject to MemeDAO governance adjustment quarterly. Creator minimum floor rate cannot go below 50% of launch rate — immutable.)*

### Likes as Micro-Payments
Every like on Sovereign Social is a **micro-INDX payment** from the viewer to the creator.

- Default like value: **0.01 INDX** (~$0.0024 USD)
- Citizens set their personal like value (min 0.001 INDX, max 1 INDX per like)
- Liking is voluntary micropayment — not forced
- Citizens with low INDX balance: likes are free (civic participation), but earn no payment for creator
- Citizens with INDX balance: default micro-payment activated, can opt out

### Subscriptions
Citizens subscribe to creators with a monthly INDX commitment:

| Tier | Monthly INDX | USD | Perks |
|------|-------------|-----|-------|
| Follower | Free | Free | Public content only |
| Supporter | 5 INDX/mo | $1.20 | All content + supporter badge |
| Champion | 20 INDX/mo | $4.80 | Exclusive content + direct message access |
| Sovereign | 50 INDX/mo | $12.00 | All above + name in credits + Wisdom boost |

98/2 applies to every subscription tier.

### Content NFT Sales
Creators can optionally list their Content NFTs for sale:

**Example:** 100 INDX earned from a collab live stream

- Creator B: 39.2 INDX (40%)

Collab content is marked with a dual-creator badge. Both audiences are notified.

### Addition 4 — Low-Bandwidth Mode (Non-Negotiable for Pacific Nations)
The Cook Islands, remote Fiji, PNG highlands, outer Tonga — internet is inconsistent, expensive, and slow. Sovereign Social must work anyway.

**Low-bandwidth protocol:**

- Video previews load at 144p by default, upgrade to 480p/720p when bandwidth allows
- SIINDEX detects connection quality on app open and activates Low-Bandwidth Mode silently
- Audio-only mode for live streams — citizen hears the stream without video bandwidth cost
- Offline post drafting — create text and image posts offline, publish when connected
- SovTokens can be queued offline — sent the moment connection restores

**SIINDEX notification when queued content publishes:** "Your video just went live. Low bandwidth mode handled it. Standing by."

This is not optional. Without this, Sovereign Social serves Sydney and Auckland, not Apia and Port Moresby.

## Gotchas (Known Edge Cases)

- **Micro-payment spam**: A creator could be sent thousands of 0.001 INDX likes by a bot. Fix: SIINDEX rate-limit detection on identical micro-transactions from single wallet.
- **Content NFT gas costs**: Minting on Solana is cheap (~$0.001) but not free. For very frequent posters, batch minting is preferable. SIINDEX batches NFT mints every 24 hours unless creator requests instant mint.
- **View reward gaming**: Bot views must be filtered. SIINDEX uses engagement quality signals (watch time, SovTokens, real follows) not raw view counts. View count displayed ≠ rewarded view count.
- **Subscription churn**: If citizen INDX balance drops below subscription cost, SIINDEX notifies both creator and subscriber 48 hours before lapse — not an instant cancellation.
- **Pacific First feed manipulation**: Creators claiming Pacific identity without being Pacific nations citizens. SIINDEX cross-references citizenship location on Grid Account to verify Pacific First eligibility.
