How does this work?
▼
IN$DEX maintains a Data Liability Pool. Companies that want to access IN$DEX citizen data must stake INDX as a liability deposit before they can participate. When a verified breach occurs, SIINDEX checks our citizens against the breached dataset. If your data was exposed, the smart contract automatically releases compensation from the staked pool to your wallet. No lawyers. No waiting. No forms. Instant.

Every claim generates an immutable ZK-proof timestamp on Solana — legal evidence that your data was breached and compensation was due.
Your legal rights: Claims are backed by GDPR Article 82 (EU right to compensation), CCPA Section 1798.150 (US right to damages), and the Australian Privacy Act 1988. IN$DEX generates a legally admissible ZK-proof for every claim filed. Compensation is paid from the Data Liability Pool — not from IN$DEX operating funds.
