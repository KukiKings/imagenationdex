God Mode Feature 2 — Status Types

SIINDEX's 8 Responses

Every SIINDEX evaluation ends in one of these statuses. Tap to expand.

✅
Safe to Proceed
Action is clear and low-risk

SIINDEX has assessed the action and found no red flags. The citizen's context, history, and the action itself all check out.

ExampleSending $50 to a known contact in a previously used corridor. Pattern matches prior behaviour. No alerts.
⚠️
Needs Approval
Citizen must explicitly confirm

The action is allowed but involves a sensitive category. SIINDEX presents a clear summary and waits for the citizen to explicitly say yes.

ExampleListing a cultural asset for sale. SIINDEX pauses to confirm: "This will be publicly visible. Is this your intention?"
📋
Needs Info
More context required

SIINDEX cannot safely proceed without additional information. She asks a targeted question — one, not many.

ExampleSend request to an unfamiliar account. SIINDEX asks: "Have you verified this recipient's details? This account hasn't been used before."
👁️
Human Review
A citizen services officer must review

Certain edge cases exceed what SIINDEX should decide alone. A human citizen services officer reviews within 24 hours.

ExampleDispute involving conflicting claims over a cultural asset between two citizens in the same community.
🌺
Cultural Review
Ancestral or community significance

Content or actions touching ancestral, ceremonial, or community-held significance are referred to the Cultural Council before proceeding.

ExampleListing an item flagged with Samoan ancestral imagery. SIINDEX pauses for cultural assessment before publication.
🛡️
Guardian Review
Guardian contact must approve

If a citizen has a Guardian Contact set up, high-risk or high-value actions notify the guardian for approval before proceeding.

ExampleCitizen in Guardian Mode attempts a $300 transfer. Guardian receives notification and must confirm.
⚖️
Legal Review
Compliance team must clear

Actions involving regulatory thresholds, novel asset types, or cross-border complexity go to IN$DEX's compliance team. AUSTRAC obligations apply.

ExampleA single transaction triggering AML thresholds. SIINDEX pauses and routes to compliance automatically.
⬛
Blocked
Cannot proceed — reason given

The action violates a hard rule — SIINDEX's constitution, IN$DEX policy, or a regulatory requirement. SIINDEX explains why and what alternatives exist.

ExampleAttempted transaction to a sanctioned address. SIINDEX blocks immediately and logs the attempt with explanation.
