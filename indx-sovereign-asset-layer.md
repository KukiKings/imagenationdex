## 5. Critical Warning — Tokenization ≠ Trust

RWA growth is exciting, but headline value is not enough.

Recent research warns that TVL or onchain asset value can hide risk, because tokenized assets may still be illiquid, concentrated, weakly traded, or dependent on offchain legal/custody structures. Research using RWA.xyz data argues that RWA assessment needs liquidity, concentration, and market-quality scoring beyond headline valuation. Tokenization and actual liquidity are distinct outcomes.

**SIINDEX must never say:**
- "Tokenized assets are automatically safer."
- "RWAs are guaranteed liquid."

**SIINDEX must say:**
> "Tokenization can make an asset easier to access and move, but it does not automatically make it safe, liquid, suitable, or fairly valued."

That becomes a **trust advantage**.

## 6. Asset Meaning Engine — SIINDEX Module Spec

Before a citizen interacts with any tokenized asset, SIINDEX explains:

### Dimension 1 — What is this asset?
Public equity, stablecoin, fund, credit product, Treasury product, cultural asset, digital collectible, marketplace asset, governance asset, or unknown.

### Dimension 2 — Who issued it?
Issuer, custodian, platform, transfer agent, legal wrapper, or unknown.

### Dimension 3 — What rights does it give?
Ownership, exposure, redemption, yield, voting, claim, access, license, or no clear right.

### Dimension 4 — Can it be redeemed?
One-for-one redemption, indirect exposure, restricted redemption, no redemption, or unknown.

### Dimension 5 — Who can hold it?
Eligible investors only, jurisdiction-restricted, KYC required, public, private, community-approved, or unknown.

### Dimension 6 — What can go wrong?
Liquidity risk, issuer risk, smart-contract risk, oracle risk, custody risk, legal risk, regulatory risk, concentration risk, market risk.

### Dimension 7 — Does it fit the citizen's mission?
Learning, saving, trading, business, cultural protection, treasury, community funding, ownership, or not suitable.

### Dimension 8 — What action is allowed?
Learn, watch, compare, prepare, request review, buy/sell with consent, or blocked.
