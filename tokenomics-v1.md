## Transaction Mechanics

### Two-Tier Transaction Tax

| Transaction Type | Tax Rate | Who it applies to |
|---|---|---|
| Platform payments (send, pay, trade) | 0.5% | Citizens using IN$DEX for real utility |
| Wallet-to-wallet token transfers | 3% | Speculative trading between wallets |

**Why two tiers:** A market vendor sending $10 to a supplier pays $0.05. Barely noticed. A trader moving 100,000 INDX between wallets pays 3% — that value flows back to citizens. Utility is cheap. Speculation funds the community.

### How the Tax Splits

Every transaction tax splits three ways:

| Destination | Share | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| Redistribution to all INDX holders | 1% | Paid instantly to every citizen holding INDX |
| Civilisation Fund | 1% | Funds buybacks, staking yield, operations |
| Permanent burn | 1% | Removed from supply forever |

The citizen who simply holds INDX earns passively from every transaction happening anywhere on the network. The more the network grows, the more every holder earns.

## Buyback & Redistribution Mechanism

**Trigger:** SIINDEX detects INDX price drops 15% or more over any 48-hour period.

**Response:**
1. Civilisation Fund activates buyback — purchases INDX from open market
2. 50% of purchased INDX redistributed equally to all holders
3. 50% burned permanently

**Effect:** When whales dump, citizens get paid. Panic selling rewards holders and reduces supply simultaneously. The floor rises over time.

**Important caveat:** The buyback only activates when the Civilisation Fund reserve holds sufficient capital. A minimum reserve threshold — equivalent to 90 days of average transaction volume — must be maintained before buyback fires. Below that threshold, the Fund accumulates. This prevents the buyback from being exploited when reserves are thin.

## Staking Model

Four tiers. Variable yield. No fixed APY promises.

| Lock Period | Fund Income Allocation | Est. APY Range | Who it's for |
|---|---|---|---|
| Flex (no lock) | 10% of monthly Fund income shared across this pool | Est. 3–5% | New citizens, not ready to commit |
| 30 days | 20% of monthly Fund income shared across this pool | Est. 4–7% | Beginners |
| 90 days | 30% of monthly Fund income shared across this pool | Est. 6–9% | Regular citizens |
| 365 days | 40% of monthly Fund income shared across this pool | Est. 9–13% | Long-term believers |
| Reserve (not distributed) | 10% held in Fund reserve | — | Buyback protection |

**Yield is estimated, not fixed.** The ranges above are illustrative based on current Fund income levels. In a high-volume month citizens earn toward the top of the range. In a quiet month they earn toward the bottom. Always real. Never promised beyond what exists.

**Why variable yield, not fixed APY:** Fixed yield promises create collapse risk — see Anchor Protocol at 20% APY on Terra. Variable yield from real platform revenue is sustainable because it only pays what the platform actually earns.

**Reward distribution:** Daily. Rewards accrue continuously and are distributed to each citizen's staking balance every 24 hours. Auto-compound reinvests daily rewards back into the same pool automatically.

**Auto-compound:** Citizens can toggle auto-compound ON. Yield payments automatically restake into the same pool. No manual action required. The position grows continuously.

**Early unstake penalty:** 2% of principal for breaking a lock before it expires. Penalty goes to the Civilisation Fund.

**Genesis token staking note:** Genesis-discounted tokens can be staked. The 90-day anti-flip lock still applies — staking does not circumvent the lock. Citizens cannot sell Genesis tokens until the lock expires, even if they are currently staked.

**Staking calculator:** Separate screen (staking-calculator.html). Citizens enter INDX amount and lock period → see estimated yield range (low/high band) for 1, 3, 5, and 10 years. Shows comparison: simple vs compound. Honest framing — range, not a fixed number.
