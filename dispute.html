🛡️ How Buyer Protection Works
1
Escrow protection: Payment is held in a smart contract escrow when you buy. The seller only receives funds when you confirm receipt.
2
Open a dispute: If something's wrong, open a dispute within 30 days. Describe the issue and upload evidence.
3
Seller responds: The seller has 72 hours to respond with their side of the story and any counter-evidence.
4
Mediation: If buyer and seller can't agree, a Wisdom Score 75+ community mediator reviews the case and decides.
5
Resolution: Escrow is released — either to the buyer (refund) or seller (payment) — based on the outcome. All on-chain, transparent.
⏱ Resolution Timeframes
Seller response: 72 hours
Mediation review: 5 business days
Refund processing: Instant (on-chain)
⚠️ Abuse Policy
False or malicious disputes reduce your Wisdom Score. Repeated abuse results in account suspension. IN$DEX protects both buyers AND sellers.
