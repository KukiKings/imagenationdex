🛡️ How Buyer Protection Works Today
1
Open a dispute: If something's wrong with a real order, open a dispute. This creates a real, permanent record — not a preview.
2
Add evidence: Describe the issue and note any evidence. It's attached to the real dispute record.
3
Resolve directly: Right now, resolution happens between buyer and seller directly (contact them outside the app) — IN$DEX keeps the record so there's a paper trail.
⚠️ Still preview (roadmap)
Smart-contract escrow that actually holds payment, automatic seller response deadlines, and Wisdom-Score-gated community mediation are not live yet. Today's version gives you a real, permanent dispute record — not automated fund holding or mediator matching.
⚠️ Abuse Policy
False or malicious disputes reduce your Wisdom Score. Repeated abuse results in account suspension. IN$DEX protects both buyers AND sellers.
