## The 7 Sovereign Revenue Streams

Every citizen has access to all 7 from the moment they join. These are not features. They are rights.

### Stream 1 — P2P Marketplace Sales
**"Sell anything. Keep 98%."**

- List a product, service, skill, or digital asset in under 60 seconds
- Scan-to-Sovereign: take a photo, tag a price, it's live
- Buyer pays in INDX — 98% goes directly to seller, 2% to Civilisation Fund
- Every sale mints an NFT receipt — immutable proof of the transaction
- Mama Noe lists her tuluk. A tourist buys it. She receives payment in 4 seconds.

**Screen:** `marketplace.html` → `create-listing.html` → `my-listings.html`

---

### Stream 2 — Data Earnings
**"Your data is being sold right now. Just not to you."**

Every major platform — Facebook, Google, TikTok, YouTube — sells citizen data to advertisers. The citizen gets nothing. IN$DEX reverses this.

- Every action on IN$DEX generates sovereign data owned by the citizen
- Citizens set a price for their data — companies pay INDX to access it
- Citizens can sell specific data sets (browsing, purchase history, preferences)
- Citizens can gate their data — nobody accesses it without paying
- ZK-proof ensures data is verified without being exposed
- Data is never stored on IN$DEX servers — it lives on the citizen's device

**Earning mechanism:** Companies pay INDX into citizen's Data Vault. Citizen approves or rejects. 98/2 Law applies — 98% to citizen, 2% Civilisation Fund.

**Screen:** `data-vault.html`

### Stream 3 — Content & Social Earnings
**"Every like, every view, every post — you get paid."**

The coconut girl posts a video of her stall. 200 people watch it. On YouTube she earns nothing. On IN$DEX:

- **Watch a video** → earn micro-INDX (funded by content creator allocation)
- **Upload content** → earn INDX when others watch, like, or share
- **Click like** → send micro-token appreciation to creator (opt-in, not mandatory)
- **Comment** → earn Wisdom Points toward Wisdom Score milestones
- **Share** → referral-style earning — if someone joins because of your content, you earn

Content lives on the sovereign mesh — censorship-proof. No platform can demonetize you. No algorithm can suppress you without your consent.

**Screen:** `feed.html` (the Earn tab)

### Stream 7 — Recursive Royalties
**"List once. Earn forever."**

Powered by Story Protocol (Wisdom Rent mechanism):

- Citizens create digital assets: templates, designs, beats, photos, documents, code
- When another citizen buys and resells that asset, original creator earns a royalty automatically
- Royalties compound: if the reseller also resells, original creator still earns
- SP-NFTs (Skill Point NFTs) track the full provenance chain on-chain
- No expiry. No platform fee. 98/2 Law applies to every royalty payment.

**Screen:** `nft-marketplace.html` → `nft-create.html`

## The Triangular Fusion Engine (Phase 3 — Sep 1–23, 2026)

The engine that turns everything above into compounding intelligence:

```
SKILL → ASSET → DATA → ENHANCED SKILL → (repeat)
```

1. **Skill Layer** — Citizen's skills are tokenized as SP-NFTs (Skill Point NFTs). Queryable by employers, clients, collaborators. The more you do, the more your skills are worth.

2. **Asset Layer** — Every economic action mints a Verifiable Receipt NFT. Her coconut sale. Her data access grant. Her content view. Every transaction = an asset.

3. **Data Layer** — The Sovereign Skill Graph. A living map of citizen capabilities and trust. Wisdom-gated API. Companies pay INDX to query it. Citizens earn.

4. **Loop** — Assets build skills. Skills earn data. Data enhances skills. Everything compounds.
