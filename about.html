Where This Started

On holiday in the Cook Islands, I watched a girl selling coconuts try to make change for $50. She couldn't. Not because she wasn't smart — because she is not the bank. I thought: what if she just scanned my free Web3 domain name ajhenry.IN$DEX QR code? She literally and instantly became the bank. That question became IN$DEX.

AJ Henry, Founder

That coconut girl has a name. I don't know it. But I think about her every day while we build.

I watched centralised exchanges fail everyday people. I watched fees eat remittances. I watched grandmothers in Samoa wait 5 days for money their children sent from Auckland.

I'm not a developer. I'm not a finance person. I'm a 52-year-old bloke from the southeastern suburbs of Melbourne, who got angry enough to do something about it.
