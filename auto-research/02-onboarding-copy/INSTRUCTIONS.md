# Experiment 02 — Onboarding Copy
## STATUS: LOCKED — Do not modify. Human eyes only.

---

## Objective

Improve the copy on the IN$DEX onboarding screens so more users complete the 3-step flow. The copy lives in `asset.json`. SCORING.py measures readability, trust signal density, and brevity.

---

## The Mama Noe Test (non-negotiable)

Every piece of copy must pass this test:

> *Can Mama Noe — 80 years old, Mele Village, Vanuatu, 50% smartphone confident — read this and know exactly what to do?*

If the answer is no, the copy fails regardless of score.

---

## What you are allowed to change

- Any value in `asset.json` under the `"copy"` key
- Headlines, subheadings, button text, helper text, error messages
- Word order, phrasing, sentence structure
- You may add or remove words, but cannot add new JSON keys

## What you must NOT change

- JSON structure or keys — only values under `"copy"`
- The IN$DEX or SIINDEX brand names
- The 3-step flow description (Step 1 = Scan & Land, Step 2 = Claim Identity, Step 3 = Wallet Live)
- Any mention that the service is free
- The `.IN$DEX` domain name format

---

## Principles for better copy

1. **Shorter is better.** The Pacific unbanked user reads on a small screen in sunlight.
2. **Action words first.** Start CTAs with a verb: "Get", "Join", "Claim", "Send".
3. **Numbers beat words.** "60 seconds" beats "very fast".
4. **Trust signals must be present** on every screen: at least one of — Free, Instant, Safe, No ID, No bank.
5. **No jargon.** "Wallet" is allowed. "Non-custodial", "Web3", "blockchain" are not.
6. **One idea per sentence.** Never two things joined with "and" in a CTA.

---

## Target

Score > 80. The score rewards clarity, trust signal density, and brevity equally.
