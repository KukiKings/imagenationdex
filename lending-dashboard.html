How Borrowing Works
1
Post USDC as collateral
Deposit USDC to back your loan. You need at least $1.50 in USDC for every $1.00 you borrow — a 150% collateral ratio.
2
SIINDEX runs pre-flight
Every borrow goes through a 7-point check: policy, security, sanctions, collateral, liquidity, limits, and the 98/2 Law. This takes under 3 seconds.
3
Receive INDX, pay interest
INDX arrives in your Grid Account. Interest accrues daily at the current borrow rate. SIINDEX watches your collateral ratio 24/7.
4
Repay to get collateral back
Repay your INDX + interest at any time. Your USDC is returned immediately upon full repayment.
