What SIINDEX does — in plain language
Why did SIINDEX freeze my account?
An automated system detected activity that matched patterns used in money laundering or fraud. It's a safety stop, not a final decision. A human reviews it within 24 hours. You can always request human review immediately.
Why did SIINDEX ask me to verify a transfer?
The amount exceeded $1,000 USD, which legally requires us to share some identifying information with the receiving platform (Travel Rule). SIINDEX protects your privacy by using a ZK-proof — sharing the minimum required, not your full details.
Why did my KYC get rejected by SIINDEX?
The biometric scan or document photo didn't meet the quality threshold, or the data didn't match. This is a technical failure, not a rejection of you. Try again in better lighting, or contact support for a manual review — no fee.
Does SIINDEX make financial decisions for me?
No. SIINDEX monitors, alerts, files reports, and blocks illegal transactions. It never buys, sells, or invests your funds without explicit instruction. You stay in control. Always.
