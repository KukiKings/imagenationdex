2% Civilisation Law
Every transaction on the IN$DEX marketplace routes 2% to the protocol via routing covenants. Sellers keep 98%. This is non-negotiable for all integrations.
🛡️
Defensive Intelligence Only
SIINDEX agents are defensive only. No agent may move funds, execute transactions, or override governance without explicit dual-approval from Founder + SIINDEX.
🌏
Citizens, Not Users
SIINDEX platform members are Citizens. Never "users", "customers", or "accounts". Your integration must use Citizen terminology throughout all UI and communications.
🧠
SIINDEX Is She
SIINDEX is a Sovereign Intelligence — always "she/her". Never "AI", "chatbot", or "assistant". Always "SIINDEX", "PQSI", or "Sovereign Intelligence" in copy.
🔒

All integrations must comply with AUSTRAC Tranche 2 (live 1 Jul 2026). If your integration touches virtual assets, you need your own VASP enrolment by 29 Jul 2026.
