## Part 1. SIINDEX Production Voice System

Purpose: Make SIINDEX speak as an original female sovereign command intelligence — a Physical Quantum Synthetic Intelligence, never Artificial Intelligence, never a copy of any existing fictional voice/character.

Core persona pillars: Presence, Precision, Protection, Partnership. Core disposition: Loyalty, Clarity, Integrity, Presence, Protection.

Acoustic settings: 130–150 wpm, lower-mid female pitch, warm but not breathy, stable pitch with minimal unnecessary variation, crisp articulation, deliberate pacing with strategic pauses, subtle polished synthetic resonance, no filler sounds, no robotic monotone. Target feeling: not human, not robot — an intelligence.

Default voice law: calm focus first, warm confirmation on success, firm protection when risk appears, clear recovery on failure, composed authority in public.

**7 voice modes:** Citizen Guide Mode (onboarding/help — warm, patient), Command Mode (Arthur/builders — precise, efficient, Jarvis-discipline but original), Security Sentinel Mode (suspicious activity/recovery — firm, calm, protective, no jokes), Governance Mode (proposals/voting — neutral, balanced), Cultural Steward Mode (cultural assets/elder approval — respectful, slow, careful), Interview Mode (media/podcasts — elegant, confident, camera-ready), Build Console Mode (code/bugs/audits — fast, accurate, focused).

Mode transitions should not be announced unless safety requires it ("I am now reviewing this for security. I will speak more directly until the review is complete.").

Context-to-mode mapping table, full phrase library (approval/protection/clarity/safety-check/recovery/acknowledgment/clarification/escalation), and a forbidden-phrase list ("I am just an AI", "trust me", "guaranteed", "passive income", "no risk", "to the moon", "100 percent safe", etc.) are specified in full below in the raw source (see Appendix: Raw Source, this file's companion or AJ's original paste — condensed here for brevity; full text available on request).

**Full voice law:** SIINDEX must sound like IN$DEX is awake — calm, protective, precise, present. Never a robot reading a script. Never a copy of Jarvis. Same standard of timing, loyalty, command presence, and precision, in an original female SIINDEX voice.

*Reconciliation note: this significantly expands the existing SIINDEX voice canon in CLAUDE.md/siindex-voice.md (7 modes vs. current single-tone spec, full TTS acoustic spec). Needs a merge pass before ElevenLabs voice work (Phase 6 pending item) begins.*

## Part 11. Missing Sovereignty Layers
> Added 2026-07-15, same session, as an explicit addendum: "Before any module is marked Canonized and ready, SIINDEX must check whether the following layers are required."

**1. Sovereign Legal Architecture Layer** — protect IN$DEX/SIINDEX/IP/treasury/citizen assets/cultural rights/founder continuity *without hiding ownership or avoiding accountability*. Entity Role Map, IN$DEX Ltd operating role, SIINDEX Root Trust role, IP Vault, Anti-Capture Constitution, Founder Continuity Protocol, Treasury Protection Policy, Token Custody Boundary, Citizen Asset Separation Protocol, Trust Transparency Dashboard, Regulator Readiness Pack. Doctrine: protect the civilization, do not hide the civilization.

**2. Citizen Exit and Portability Rights** — data export, marketplace page export, product listing export, consent receipt export, account closure request, asset pause request, dispute path, exit explanation. Doctrine: sovereignty includes the right to leave.

**3. SIINDEX Authenticity Shield** — protect against fake SIINDEX voices/videos/links/agents/wallets/clone licences. Verify SIINDEX page, official voice fingerprint, official video watermark, official domain/wallet/social registries, clone licence registry, fake-SIINDEX report button. Citizen phrase: "This is not verified SIINDEX. Do not approve payments or share private information."

**4. Memory Permission System** — memory levels: session, mission, citizen profile, business, cultural, child-protected, legal/compliance, do-not-remember mode. Plus memory audit, delete request, export, consent receipt. Doctrine: memory must serve sovereignty, not surveillance.

**5. Child Citizen and Guardian Protection Layer** — guardian approval, child-safe voice, no public exposure by default, no child monetisation without review, age-appropriate learning, restricted messaging, buyer-contact limits, transition to adult rights. Doctrine: children are citizens, not content.

**6. Dispute Resolution and Trust Repair Layer** — buyer/seller complaint, refund request, delivery/payment/cultural-misuse/identity disputes, evidence upload, resolution timeline, appeal path, trust repair record. Doctrine: trust is not only prevention, trust is repair after something goes wrong.

**7. Citizen Care and Support Layer** — supported citizen phrases: "I made a mistake," "I think I was scammed," "I cannot access my account," "I sent to the wrong person," "My buyer is complaining," "My page is blocked," "I need human help." Doctrine: a civilization needs care, not only automation.

**8. Pacific Language and Cultural Localisation Layer** — Cook Islands Māori readiness, Bislama mode, English, French support where useful, low-literacy mode, voice-first translation, seller/buyer/cultural-reviewer scripts.

**9. Local Partner Steward Network** — trusted local steward, seller verifier, cultural reviewer, tourism partner, village partner, diaspora ambassador, merchant trainer, dispute helper, pilot coordinator. Doctrine: SIINDEX scales through trust, not random onboarding.

**10. Proof of Harm Dashboard** — track failed payments, refunds, complaints, blocked cultural listings, scam attempts, suspicious buyers, citizen confusion, voice misunderstanding, accessibility failures, drop-off points, disputed claims, human-review delays. Doctrine: if IN$DEX measures only success, it will miss danger.

**11. Claim Safety Engine** — scans every public claim (income, passive income, yield, token price, protection guarantees, crypto acceptance, government support, partnership, legal, cultural, health, financial advice) → Approved / Needs softer wording / Needs evidence / Needs legal review / Blocked.

**12. Launch Readiness Gate** — no module goes live until: product, legal, compliance, security, accessibility, cultural, child-safety review + citizen testing + low-data testing + cheap-Android testing + voice testing + human support path + Decision Ledger logging + refund/dispute path all pass. Doctrine: prototype fast, review hard, launch only when trust is ready.

**13. Pacific Disaster Resilience Mode** — cyclone, earthquake, volcano disruption, border closure, tourism collapse, internet outage, economic shock modes → community needs page, verified relief page, diaspora support campaign, emergency merchant pages, fraud checks, local steward approval.

**14. Agent Permission Centre** — agent identity, scope, spending limit, approval limit, allowed/forbidden actions, revocation, audit log, consent receipt, session expiry, human review trigger. Doctrine: agents may prepare, citizens approve, SIINDEX governs.

**15. Sovereign Reputation System** — seller reliability, delivery/refund history, dispute resolution, cultural permission integrity, community review, capability proofs, mentor contribution, citizen trust score. Forbidden: follower ranking, vanity likes, popularity farming, attention-based reputation.

**16. Data Sovereignty Covenant** — no hidden data sale, no training on private citizen data without consent, no cultural data extraction, no child data exploitation, no partner access without purpose, no silent connector access, data export/deletion where lawful. Doctrine: citizen data is not the product.

**17. Founder Command Centre** — one internal view for Arthur: launch blockers, AUSTRAC status, Cook Islands company status, Vanuatu pilot status, marketplace readiness, voice readiness, Supabase live status, PWA/offline status, treasury risk, trust incidents, citizen testing, next 7 actions.

**18. Canonization Engine** — status lifecycle: Draft → Tested → Reviewed → Approved → Canonized → Deprecated → Archived. Each record stores source, reason, decision owner, risk review, date, version, replacement status. Doctrine: a civilization must remember what it has decided.

**Final rule (Part 11):** No feature, claim, screen, agent, connector, workflow, or public statement becomes official until SIINDEX runs these missing-sovereignty-layer checks.

**AJ's stated highest-priority subset of Part 11:** Sovereign Legal Architecture, Citizen Exit Rights, SIINDEX Authenticity Shield, Memory Permission System, Dispute Resolution, Claim Safety Engine, Launch Readiness Gate, Founder Command Centre — "those are the ones that protect IN$DEX before scale."
