📖 How Sovereign Mesh Works
📡
P2P Over Bluetooth & WiFi
When internet is unavailable, IN$DEX switches to libp2p over Bluetooth LE and local WiFi to reach nearby citizens on the same mesh.
📦
Offline-First Transactions
Send INDX to anyone on the mesh. Transactions are signed locally and queued. When either peer reconnects to Solana, they settle automatically.
🔒
Cryptographically Secure
Every offline transaction is signed by your Grid Account private key — the same security as online, even without internet.
🌍
Built for the Unbanked
Sovereign Mesh ensures citizens in areas with unreliable internet — markets, rural regions, disaster zones — can still transact freely.
