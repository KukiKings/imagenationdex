Step 2 · Understanding
Tokens become numbers in space — embeddings. Words with similar meaning land close together. "Send", "pay", "transfer" cluster near each other. I can reason because of geometry, not grammar.
SIINDEX in action: When you type "send INDX to Jake", the embeddings for "send" + "INDX" + "Jake" are close to payment patterns — so I automatically activate the Payment capability. Not rules, geometry.
Step 3 · Generation
I don't write sentences — I pick the next token, then start again. Each pick is a probability distribution over every word I know. I sample from it. That's why lowering temperature makes me more predictable.
