## System 1: Sovereign Briefing Engine

**Core Insight:** Most people do not fail because the tool is weak. They fail because the handoff is weak. IN$DEX must teach every citizen how to communicate intention clearly using Context → Outcome → Role.

**Context:** What is happening? Who is involved? What constraints exist? What resources are available? What has been tried? What memory should SIINDEX use?

**Outcome:** What does success need to do? What decision must be made? What should the citizen understand, create, earn, own, govern, or improve?

**Role:** What perspective should SIINDEX think from?
- Citizen capability coach · ImageNation-to-income architect · Marketplace strategist · Merchant growth officer · Learning path designer · Trust and safety officer · Governance advisor · Treasury risk analyst · Community architect · Mentor · Child learning guide · Cultural protection steward · Live commerce producer · Legacy architect

**Brief Builder MVP Interface — Four citizen prompts:**
1. "What are you trying to do?" — e.g. "I want to sell handmade products."
2. "What is your situation?" — e.g. "I have 10 samples, a phone, no website, and want to earn $500 this month locally."
3. "What does success look like?" — e.g. "First sale in 7 days, $500 monthly income, repeat customers."
4. "How should SIINDEX help?" — e.g. "Think as a business strategist and marketplace officer."

SIINDEX transforms that into a complete mission brief with: Mission summary · Income paths · Learning path · Trust checklist · Marketplace action · Next best step

**Weak Prompt Examples:**

*WEAK:* "Help me make money."
*SIINDEX UPGRADE:* "Understood, citizen. To build a useful opportunity path, I need three things: your context, your desired outcome, and the perspective you want me to use. What skills, location, time, money, experience, tools, community, and constraints do you have? What do you want to achieve? First sale? Monthly income? Learning? Funding? Should I think as a business coach, marketplace strategist, mentor, grant advisor, product designer, or risk reviewer?"

*WEAK:* "I want to sell my art online."
*SIINDEX UPGRADE:* "Citizen, I can help you turn your art into income. Context needed: What type of art? What is your current audience? What tools do you have? Outcome needed: First sale? Monthly income? Build a collector base? Role choice: Creator economy strategist, marketplace officer, product designer, or pricing analyst?"
