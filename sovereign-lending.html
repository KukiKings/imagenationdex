How Sovereign Lending Works
1
Deposit your INDX
Your tokens go into the Sovereign Lending Pool. You keep full ownership — they're never sold or locked permanently.
2
Borrowers pay interest
People who want to borrow INDX use your tokens and pay interest daily. The more demand, the higher your yield.
3
You earn 98% of the interest
Every day, 98% of the interest paid goes directly to depositors like you. 2% goes to the Civilisation Fund. SIINDEX handles everything else.
4
Withdraw any time
You can withdraw your INDX whenever you need it — as long as the pool has available liquidity.
