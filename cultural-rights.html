⚠️ What SIINDEX Will Never Allow
Commercialising a cultural asset without confirmed permission — regardless of how it was obtained.
Using Sacred or Restricted knowledge in marketplace listings, media kits, or public posts.
Attributing cultural ownership incorrectly — including claiming personal ownership of community knowledge.
Bypassing elder or community review where it is required.
Digitising or archiving cultural knowledge without consent from its rightful holders.
